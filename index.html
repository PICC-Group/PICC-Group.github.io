The PICC Group

2024 IEEE AP-S Student Design Contest winners representing Lund University.

The Polarisation and Impedance Controlled Car (PICC) is a device that utilises on-body antennas and a NanoVNA to showcase how impedance and polarisation can be used to control a RC car. If you want to learn more check out the papers section.
